    When the connection comes, this Plug shall seek for the authorization headers and set the authorized DataBase.Schemas.Account.t/0 into our Absinthe context.

    The connection flow isn’t affected. It’s up to the Absinthe resolver to decide what to do next.

    The following headers are inspected:

    • account-id
    • api-token
