    Amethyst vdev DataBase.Schemas.AccountTransfer View Source

    The centralized agreement for exchanging assets between two distinct accounts.

    A transfer between accounts is performed by creating a outbound movement to the sender account and a inbound movement to the recipient account.

    This module provides a function to register those two DataBase.Schemas.AccountMovement.t/0 as being part of a formal transfer.

    See register/2.

    Expresses information over the account_transfers database table.
