    Amethyst vdev DataBase.Schemas.AccountHistory View Source

    The bank account daily logbook.

    It’s in charge to keep track of all DataBase.Schemas.AccountMovement.t/0 :direction and :amount of a DataBase.Schemas.Account.t/0 for each day.

    It acts as logbook. Expresses information over the account_history database table. Has its :account_id and :date acting both as a composite primary key.

    Provides functions to both register and report informations.
